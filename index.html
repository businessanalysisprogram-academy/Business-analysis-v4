Everything: Business Analysis training, 20+ AI tools mastery, a project based learning internship with certificate, live classes, one-to-one evaluation, and full career support — resume building, LinkedIn optimization, interview prep, mock interviews, placement assistance and job alerts. A few hours of live classes plus practice and project work. The pace fits alongside studies or a job, taking you from zero to job-ready in around 60 days.
